Welcome to Site-OC-01

Hi, my name is Occupy and I am actively working on a project inspired by the SCP Foundation, though not affiliated.

This will mainly be an XR or VR project, and I will now go further in depth about this:

  • This will mainly be a horror-focused game/project.
  • It will take a while to polish and get ready.
  • There are multiple 'wings' to the facility site in VR, and I will now go further in depth:
  • TOG-Alpha: Admin / Research Hubs that include labs housing the latest technology and rooms where anomalies are reviewed as a way to prepare for cataloging.
  • TOG-Bravo: Experimental labs that experiment on weird specimens and anomalies while still under containment.
  • TOG-Charlie: A containment wing with high-risk anomalous entities with containment cells, observation areas, and emergency lockdowns if necessary.
  • TOG-Delta: Surveillance / Security operations areas which include monitoring stations, patrol routes, and security points.
  • TOG-Echo: Maintenance / Reactor areas which include environmental hazards, and essential systems outlined for operations floor plan.
  • TOG-Zeta: Quarantine wing for isolated, highly unstable anomalous entities—mostly each other and redundant for containment.

This will be a solo dev project and WILL release on the Meta store. The project is most likely releasing very late 2025 or early 2026. See you all until then! I will be posting updates on the "Updates" page.
